That Chilling Night.

Smile came on my face , when she knocked outside my window;
Blowing air to her hands , to overcome this killing cold.
Her helplessness was outshowing from her beautiful face,
I could do anything for her smile till my last race.
My eyes sticked to her green bordered blue bold dress.

Her trilling body with full of innocence and simplicity,
Pinched my heart to make her wait of every microseconds.
Hugged me with her full strength and effort that she keeps,
Confused me whether to show her emotion or to have warmness,
Whatever it be there were some ways to show happiness.

Sat on my reddish-brown leather couch with a soft blanket,
Waiting for me to join , to spend this coolest night.
Moving her head here and there to have some playful stuffs,
Unable to see anything as darkness was around us,
A piano started right behind from a red white toy bus.

She remained silent and calm as if like a monument,
Enjoying the soft music , covering the silence around us.
Dancing of the burning wood's flames on the music beats,
May be my illusion or the reality happening in my den.
Darkness , music and she ; Oh , what a lovely combination!

With a soft fall of note , I ended with a small hesitaion.
No idea how to proceed in this difficult situation,
But my legs started to the couch , with a slow motion.
I rested my back against that couch , with a speechless tone,
Second time in my life , who could mute me by muting herself.

None of us wanted to break this mysterious silence,
Eyes were only medium of our conversation for that night .
The soft yellow light glowed her face , coming from the burning flames,
However also reached to my heart when I saw again her beautiful face.
Oh! What a lovely night , Oh! What a lovely night.

But one thing listen ,Oh!my dear.
Darkness is a boon for those,
Who give up their life to silence.
And a curse for those,
Who never have made the noise of silence.
